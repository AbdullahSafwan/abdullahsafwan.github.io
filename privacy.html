Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 1, 2025

Introduction

This privacy policy explains how Abdullah Safwan ("I", "me", or "my") collects, uses, and protects your information when you visit this portfolio website. I am committed to transparency about data collection practices and compliance with applicable privacy laws including GDPR, CCPA, and other regulations.

Key Points:

  • I collect pseudonymous data (data that could identify you with additional information) through cookies and analytics
  • I require your explicit consent before collecting analytics data
  • I do not collect your name, email address, or other directly identifying information through analytics
  • Your data is processed by third-party services including Google Analytics
  • You have the right to withdraw consent and request data deletion at any time

Information I Collect

Analytics Data (Pseudonymous)

With your consent, I use Google Analytics to collect pseudonymous data about how visitors interact with this website. While I cannot personally identify you by name, the following data is collected:

  • Client ID: A unique identifier stored in a cookie that tracks your sessions on this website
  • IP Address: Your IP address is sent to Google Analytics and then anonymized (last octet removed)
  • Browser fingerprint: Browser type, version, operating system, screen resolution, language, and timezone
  • Behavioral data: Pages visited, time spent on pages, buttons clicked, navigation patterns, and file downloads
  • Geographic location: Approximate location at city/region level derived from your IP address
  • Referral source: How you found this website (e.g., search engine, direct link, social media)
  • Device information: Device type (mobile, desktop, tablet) and manufacturer

Important: Under GDPR and other privacy laws, this data is considered personal data because it relates to an identifiable person (via the Client ID cookie and other identifiers). However, I do not collect your name, email address, or other directly identifying information, and I cannot personally identify specific individuals from my analytics dashboard.

Necessary Cookies

I use essential cookies to remember your preferences, such as your theme selection (light/dark mode) and cookie consent choices. These cookies are stored locally in your browser and do not track you across websites.

How I Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely to:

  • Understand how visitors use my portfolio
  • Improve the website experience
  • Track which sections are most viewed
  • Analyze traffic patterns

Data Sharing

I do not sell or trade your personal information. However, the following data sharing occurs:

  • Google Analytics: Your data is processed by Google LLC (a third party) in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. This includes transferring data to Google's servers, which may be located outside your country.
  • IP Anonymization: While IP addresses are anonymized before storage, Google initially receives your full IP address for geolocation purposes before removing the last octet.

Google Analytics uses this data to provide me with aggregated reports and may use it to improve their own services. You can learn more about how Google uses data at How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Accept or reject analytics cookies at any time
  • Change your cookie preferences using the banner that appears on first visit
  • Request information about data collected (contact me via email)

Important: Data Access and Deletion Limitations

Please note: While Google Analytics collects pseudonymous data (Client ID, cookies, behavioral patterns), I do not have the ability to link this data to your real-world identity (name, email, etc.) unless you provide me with specific information about your visit.

If you request access to your data:

I can provide:

  • Aggregated statistics about overall site traffic and popular pages
  • General analytics reports for specific time periods (if you provide visit dates/times and approximate location)
  • Information about what data is collected as outlined in this policy
  • Your Google Analytics Client ID (if you provide it from your browser's cookies)

I cannot provide:

  • Individual session data without additional identifying information from you (I cannot link Client IDs to real people)
  • Complete browsing history unless you provide specific session identifiers
  • Data that directly identifies you by name (this is never collected)

If you request deletion of your data:

Due to the pseudonymous nature of Google Analytics data and the lack of User-ID tracking, I cannot identify and delete your specific data without your Google Analytics Client ID. However, you can:

  • Clear your browser cookies to remove the Client ID and prevent future tracking
  • Use the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • Withdraw consent using the cookie preferences on this website (prevents future data collection)
  • Provide your Client ID (found in browser developer tools) for targeted deletion via Google's User Deletion API

All analytics data is automatically deleted after Google Analytics' retention period (14-26 months by default).

Data Retention

Analytics data is retained according to Google Analytics' default retention settings (typically 14-26 months). Cookie consent preferences are stored locally in your browser.

Third-Party Services

This website uses the following third-party services that may process your data:

  • Google Analytics (Google LLC): For website analytics with IP anonymization. Google may process your data in accordance with their privacy policy and may transfer data internationally.
  • GitHub Pages (GitHub, Inc.): For website hosting. GitHub may log IP addresses and other connection information for security and operational purposes.
  • Google Fonts (Google LLC): For typography. When you visit this site, Google may receive your IP address and browser information to serve font files.
  • Font Awesome CDN (Fonticons, Inc.): For icons. Font Awesome may receive your IP address when loading icon resources.
  • jsDelivr CDN: For the cookie consent library. jsDelivr may log your IP address when serving files.

Each of these services has their own privacy policies and data processing practices. I do not control how these third parties process your data beyond the configuration options I've implemented (such as IP anonymization for Google Analytics).
